The Powerhouse

SwiftInvoice Data Studio

Invoicing software usually stops at the PDF. We don't. Data Studio is a powerful analytics dashboard built directly into the app—no monthly fees, no data uploads.

Bank-Grade Encryption

Your financial data is sensitive. That's why SwiftInvoice allows you to lock your backups with AES-256 encryption before they ever leave your browser.

  • Client-side cryptography using crypto-js.
  • Password-derived keys ensure only you can access your history.
  • Decrypt files locally to restore your workspace instantly.

Algorithmic Executive Summaries

You don't need a data scientist. SwiftInvoice generates a plain-English summary of your business performance automatically.

  • Financial Velocity: Tracks revenue growth vs. previous periods.
  • Market Dynamics: Analyzes client concentration and effective tax rates.
  • Operational Rhythm: Identifies your busiest hours and days.

Deep Visual Analytics

Move beyond spreadsheets. Visualize your revenue streams with interactive charts powered by Chart.js.

  • Loyalty Tracking: Distinguish revenue from New vs. Returning clients.
  • Heatmaps: See exactly when your business makes money (Hourly/Weekly).
  • Raw Data Access: Inspect the full JSON structure of your backups.
